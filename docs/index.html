Nutritional Overview

This collection prioritizes high-protein, vegetarian ingredients. The table below summarizes key protein sources covered in these pages.

Ingredient Protein (per 100g) Calories Notes
Firm Tofu (pressed) 8g 76 kcal Complete protein; freeze to boost to ~11g
Firm Tofu (frozen + pressed) ~11g ~105 kcal Recommended prep for max protein density
Paneer 18g 265 kcal High protein, high calcium; use in moderation
High-protein tip: Always freeze and press tofu before marinating — it removes water and concentrates protein per serving. Pair paneer or tofu with beans or vegetables to build a complete, balanced meal.
