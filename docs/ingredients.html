Za'atar In stock Aromatic / Spice ~300 ~10g Calcium, iron, thymol (antioxidant) Blend of thyme, sumac, sesame, salt. Herby, tangy, nutty. Best added at the end or as a finishing sprinkle.

White pepper In stock Aromatic / Spice 296 10g Manganese, iron Subtler, more floral heat than black pepper. Preferred in light-colored sauces and dairy marinades where black specks are unwanted.
Beets In stock Vegetable Sweet 43 1.6g Folate (27% DV), potassium, nitrates Earthy, sweet. Nitrates support blood pressure and endurance. Roasting concentrates sweetness; boiling loses nutrients to water.
Kale In stock Vegetable 49 4.3g Vitamin K (684% DV), vitamin C, calcium, iron One of the most nutrient-dense leafy greens. Bitter raw; massage with oil or blanch to mellow. High oxalates — rotate with spinach.
Celery In stock Vegetable Aromatic / Spice 16 0.7g Vitamin K, folate, potassium Aromatic base vegetable (mirepoix). Very low calorie. High water content adds bulk to soups and salads.
Spinach In stock Vegetable 23 2.9g Vitamin K (460% DV), folate, iron, magnesium Milder than kale. Wilts quickly — good for cooked dishes. High oxalates reduce iron absorption; pair with vitamin C source.
Sweet potato In stock Sweet / Starchy Vegetable 86 1.6g Beta-carotene (Vit A, 284% DV), potassium, fiber Naturally sweet, filling. Excellent for balancing spicy or bitter flavors. Roast for sweetness; steam to preserve nutrients.
Brussels sprouts In stock Vegetable 43 3.4g Vitamin C (142% DV), vitamin K, folate, glucosinolates Bitter, nutty when roasted. Glucosinolates are anti-cancer compounds. High heat (roast, char) transforms bitterness into sweetness.
Miso paste In stock Umami Fermented / Probiotic 199 12g Manganese, copper, B vitamins; ~3700mg sodium/100g Deep savory base. White miso is mildest; red is strongest. Don't boil — destroys probiotics. Pairs well with acid and dairy.
Kimchi In stock Fermented / Probiotic Acid 15 1.1g Vitamin C, probiotics, capsaicin Spicy, sour, funky. A condiment and flavor bomb in one. Add at the end of cooking to preserve live cultures.
Pickles In stock Acid Fermented / Probiotic 11 0.3g Probiotics (if lacto-fermented), sodium Sour, salty, crunchy. Brine is useful as a marinade acid or seasoning liquid. Check labels — vinegar pickles have no live cultures.
Lemon juice In stock Acid 22 0.4g Vitamin C (28% DV/100ml) Bright, clean acid. Brightens virtually any dish. Helps iron absorption from plant foods. Add at the end to preserve flavor.
Balsamic vinegar In stock Acid Sweet 88 0.5g Polyphenols, antioxidants, potassium Sweet-tart, syrupy. Use sparingly in marinades — can dominate and stain. Reduces beautifully as a glaze. Pairs with roasted vegetables.
Labneh In stock Fat / Richness Fermented / Probiotic Protein base 167 11g Calcium, probiotics, B12 Strained yogurt — thick, tangy, rich. Excellent marinade base; chars well at high heat. Can be used as a spread or dip.
Kefir In stock Fermented / Probiotic Protein base 60 3.3g Probiotics (30+ strains), B12, calcium, phosphorus Drinkable fermented milk, tangier than yogurt. Thins thick dairy bases like labneh in marinades. Higher probiotic diversity than yogurt.
Soy milk In stock Protein base 54 3.3g Calcium (fortified), vitamin D, isoflavones Highest protein of all plant milks. Neutral flavor. Good for smoothies, sauces, and cooking where dairy is avoided.
Pesto In stock Fat / Richness Umami ~340 ~6g Vitamin K, olive oil monounsaturated fats, basil antioxidants Ready-made flavor base — oil, basil, cheese, nuts. Use as a marinade, sauce, or finishing condiment. High calorie; a little goes far.
Liquid aminos In stock Umami ~40 ~5g ~320mg sodium/½ tbsp; all essential amino acids Soy-derived, gluten-free, milder than soy sauce. Adds savory depth without dominating. Pairs well with miso — use together at reduced quantities.
Mango pickle chutney In stock Sweet Acid ~150 ~1g Vitamin C, beta-carotene, sodium (varies by brand) Sweet, sour, spiced. Strong flavor — use as a condiment, glaze, or marinade addition. Good counterpoint to bitter greens and rich cheese.
Lentils In stock Protein base Sweet / Starchy 116 9g Folate (45% DV), iron (18% DV), fiber (7.9g), potassium One of the best plant protein sources. Red lentils dissolve into soups and dal; green/brown hold their shape. Pair with vitamin C to boost iron absorption.

Rich, earthy umami — one of the most flavorful mushroom varieties. Roast or pan-fry at high heat to char edges. Beta-glucans support immune function.
Sesame seeds Out of stock Fat / Richness Aromatic / Spice 573 17g Calcium (88% DV/100g), iron, zinc, sesamol (antioxidant) Nutty, toasty. One of the best plant sources of calcium. Used as a finishing texture and in za'atar. Base for tahini.
Coconut flakes Out of stock Fat / Richness Sweet 660 6.7g MCT (medium-chain triglycerides), manganese, iron Sweet, tropical, rich. MCTs are digested quickly for energy. Use unsweetened. Good in smoothies, granola, and Southeast Asian dishes.
Peanut butter Out of stock Fat / Richness Protein base 588 25g Niacin (B3), vitamin E, magnesium, monounsaturated fats Highest protein of the nut butters. Pairs with soy/tamari in sauces. Use natural (no added oil/sugar) for savory applications.
